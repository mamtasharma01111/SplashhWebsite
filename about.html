Our Story

Located in the heart of Begusarai, Splashh Resort & Banquet is a luxury-budget hotel that combines comfort and style. Whether you're hosting an engagement, wedding, or other events, our beautiful event spaces provide the perfect backdrop for your special moments with loved ones.

Our versatile venues can accommodate 100 to 1000 guests, making us ideal for both small and large gatherings.

Every aspect of Splashh Resort & Banquet has been carefully crafted to ensure a comfortable, enriching, and luxurious experience
